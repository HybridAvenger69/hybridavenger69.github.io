theirs also a little bit of sorcery (magic) so you can learn how to give up your blood to the demons of the nether in order to gain special item that you can use to progress even further in making what new armor set or machine or item it is you are aiming to make

https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/masstech-enchanced

A Quest Based Expert Pack Designed to Put you through all the Mods. Loot Crates themselves can be bought from the quest book store, each of them will have different items(EX- Epic Crafts could have certain items that u can or cant craft) With FTB Money, The Amount you will get from each quest will variate on how hard the quest is Also A few mods will help with fps and other fixes needed. More Will come as time needs it. The Pack will be gated, Which means that you have to do one mod to get into another mod(example: Lets say you want to get into thermal expansion, you would need to do something in another mod to get it) There is 100+ Mods in the pack. More To Come. I would recommend at least 4gb just for base stuff if u add texturepacks make it at least 6-7gbs depending on the texture pack I do ask that if u find any issues please put it in the issues page or on the discord, this does include duping bugs and glitches, any crashing.   Like all my other packs you will have a lot of items to build with, but unlike any of em you cant start the way you wanna startwith being u gonna have to go through each mod.
